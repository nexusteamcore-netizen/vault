Common Queries

How does PhantomAPI encrypt my secrets?

Every secret is encrypted using AES-256-GCM with a unique initialization vector (IV) before being stored. This means even if our database were compromised, your secrets remain completely unreadable.

How does the MCP integration work?

PhantomAPI runs a native MCP (Model Context Protocol) server. Configure your AI agent (Claude, Cursor, etc.) to point at your PhantomAPI MCP endpoint and it can securely request any secret by name — no tokens in prompts.

Can I use PhantomAPI in my existing CI/CD pipeline?

Yes. Use our REST API or CLI to inject secrets at build time. No more .env files or hardcoded credentials in your pipeline configs. Secrets are fetched on demand and never cached to disk.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately with no downtime.

Do you offer an SLA for Enterprise?

Yes. Enterprise customers receive a 99.99% uptime SLA, dedicated support, and custom onboarding to meet compliance requirements.
